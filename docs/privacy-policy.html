Privacy Policy for SmartNotes

Last updated: July 22, 2026

SmartNotes ("the App", "we", "us") is a note-taking app with AI-assisted features (summaries, writing enhancement, and translation), voice recording, photo capture, and drawing. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it's used, and your choices.

Information We Collect

DataPurpose
Account info (name, email, profile photo) via Google or Apple Sign-In Authenticate you and sync your notes across devices
Notes content — text, voice recordings, photos, drawings, checklists Stored so you can access and sync your notes (Firebase Firestore & Cloud Storage)
Note text you submit to an AI action (Summarize, Enhance, Translate) Sent to Google's Gemini API to generate the requested result. Not used to train AI models by us.
Subscription & purchase status Processed via RevenueCat and Apple's App Store to manage your Pro subscription and usage limits

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication, database (Firestore), and file storage
  • Google Generative AI (Gemini) — powers AI Summarize/Enhance/Translate features
  • RevenueCat — subscription management
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — in-app purchase processing

Each of these providers processes data under their own privacy policies. We do not sell your personal data to third parties or use it for advertising.

Data Storage & Security

Your notes and account data are stored securely in Firebase, access-controlled so only your authenticated account can read or modify your own notes.

Your Choices

  • You can delete individual notes at any time from within the App.
  • To request full account and data deletion, contact us at the email below.
  • AI features are optional — the App is fully usable for manual note-taking without ever using Summarize, Enhance, or Translate.

Children's Privacy

SmartNotes is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
