Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do AI features work? Summarize, Enhance, and Translate use AI to process the text of a note you select. Free accounts get a limited number of uses per month; Pro subscribers get a much higher monthly/yearly limit.
  • How do I restore a purchase? Go to Settings → Manage Subscription → Restore, or tap Restore on the Upgrade to Pro screen.
  • How do I lock a note? Long-press a note and choose Lock — it will require Face ID to open.
  • My notes aren't syncing. Make sure you're signed in and have an internet connection. If the issue persists, contact us at the email above.
